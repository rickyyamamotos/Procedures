Names:
	-Multi-Domain (SAN) Certificate
	-Subject Alternative Names (SANs)
	-Unified Communication Certificate (UCC) SSLs.

Advantages:
-securing multiple names across different domains and subdomains (e.g., Exchange/OCS environments). 
-Add, change, or delete a SAN at any point during the lifecycle of the certificate for no extra charge.
-Wildcards can be added as domains in multi-domain certificates or Unified Communications Certificates (UCC).
-Protect up to 100 websites.
-Godaddy offers it. Godaddy price is for 5 websites, more websites for an additional fee.


Example:
www.example.com
www.example2.com
www.example3.net
mail.example.net
