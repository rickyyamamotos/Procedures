Zendesk contact not launching

-Go to 3CX Web client\Option (gear icon)\Advanced Settings\Integration\General\
-In the Maximum digit length to compare, enter 10 or 11
-Check "Create new contact if not found" (optional)
-Save
-Go to 3CX Web client\Option (gear icon)\Advanced Settings\Integration\Zendesk\
-Determine the end-user default web browser
-Determine the end-user web-browser used with Zendesk
-If both browser are not the same, you need to uncheck "Use default browser" and specify the executable of the browser used by zendesk
-Save
-Test
