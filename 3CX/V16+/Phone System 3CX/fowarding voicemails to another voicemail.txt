To Forward a Copy of One of your Voice Messages to Another peson's Voicemail, using Your Phone or 3CX Client (computer App) 
This procedure is similar to the Voicemail usage on all<Site>le phones.

1) Call 999 to access your voicemail
2) enter your 4 digits Voicelmail ID/PIN
3) Press * to listen to your voice messages
4) Browse your messages
4) When you listen to a message that you want to forward, press 5
5) Enter the extension of the person that you want to receive a copy of your voicemail
6) Done

This is the only way to forward a voice message
